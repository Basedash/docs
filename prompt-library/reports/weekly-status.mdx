title: 'Weekly company status update'
description: 'Comprehensive overview of key business metrics and activities'
---

Get a comprehensive weekly overview of everything happening at your company. This report provides executives and team leads with a high-level view of business performance across all key areas.

```
Generate a comprehensive weekly status report covering:

1. User Growth: New signups, active users, and growth rate compared to previous week
2. Revenue Metrics: Total revenue, MRR/ARR changes, and top revenue-generating customers
3. Product Usage: Most used features, engagement metrics, and any notable usage patterns
4. Customer Health: New customers, churned customers, and customers at risk
5. Support & Operations: Support ticket volume, average response time, and critical issues
6. Team Activity: Key accomplishments, deployments, and upcoming priorities

Include week-over-week comparisons and highlight any significant changes or anomalies that require attention. Format the report with clear sections and actionable insights.
```
