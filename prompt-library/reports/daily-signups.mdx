---
title: 'Daily user signup tracking'
description: 'Monitor new user registrations and activation rates'
---

Track daily user signups and early engagement metrics to quickly identify trends and issues. This report helps growth teams stay on top of acquisition performance and activation rates.

```
Create a daily report on user signups that includes:

1. Total new signups in the past 24 hours
2. Comparison to the same day last week and 30-day average
3. Signup sources (organic, paid, referral, etc.) with conversion rates
4. Geographic distribution of new signups
5. Activation rate: percentage of new users who completed key onboarding steps
6. Notable signups: any high-value companies or users matching our ICP
7. Signup quality indicators: email domain types, company sizes, industries

Flag any unusual patterns such as signup spikes, drops, or changes in source distribution. Include a 7-day trend chart.
```
