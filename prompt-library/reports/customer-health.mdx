---
title: 'Customer health monitoring'
description: 'Identify at-risk customers and expansion opportunities'
---

Proactively monitor customer health to reduce churn and identify expansion opportunities. This report helps customer success teams prioritize their efforts and take action before customers leave.

```
Generate a customer health report analyzing:

1. At-Risk Customers:
   - Customers with declining usage (30%+ drop in past 30 days)
   - Customers who haven't logged in for 14+ days
   - Customers with open support tickets or negative feedback
   - Customers approaching renewal with low engagement
   - For each, include: account name, MRR, risk factors, and recommended actions

2. Healthy & Growing Customers:
   - Customers with increasing usage trends
   - High engagement scores and positive feedback
   - Customers using advanced features
   - Potential expansion opportunities based on usage patterns

3. Onboarding Status:
   - New customers (first 90 days) and their progress
   - Customers stuck in onboarding
   - Time to value metrics

4. Overall Health Metrics:
   - Distribution of customers by health score (healthy, at-risk, critical)
   - Churn risk by segment
   - Net Revenue Retention trends

Prioritize the top 10 accounts requiring immediate attention and provide specific next steps for the customer success team.
```
