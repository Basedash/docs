---
title: 'Weekly sales performance report'
description: 'Track sales team performance and pipeline health'
---

Monitor sales team performance, pipeline health, and revenue trends. This report helps sales leaders identify top performers, coaching opportunities, and potential issues before they impact revenue.

```
Create a weekly sales performance report that includes:

1. Revenue Summary:
   - Total revenue closed this week vs. target
   - Month-to-date revenue vs. monthly goal
   - Quarter-to-date revenue vs. quarterly goal

2. Pipeline Health:
   - Total pipeline value by stage
   - Number of opportunities in each stage
   - Average deal size and sales cycle length
   - Pipeline coverage ratio (pipeline value / quota)

3. Team Performance:
   - Revenue by sales rep with rankings
   - Number of deals closed per rep
   - Win rate by rep
   - Activity metrics: calls made, meetings held, emails sent

4. Deal Analysis:
   - Largest deals closed this week
   - Deals at risk of slipping
   - Stalled opportunities (no activity in 14+ days)

5. Trends & Insights:
   - Week-over-week changes in key metrics
   - Top performing industries or customer segments
   - Common objections or loss reasons

Highlight any reps who need support and celebrate wins. Include actionable recommendations for improving performance.
```
