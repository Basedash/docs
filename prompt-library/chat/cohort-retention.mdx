---
title: 'Cohort retention analysis'
description: 'Analyze user retention by signup cohort'
---

Understand how well you're retaining users over time by analyzing retention rates for different signup cohorts. This prompt provides insights into which cohorts have the best retention and helps identify patterns.

```
Analyze user retention by signup month cohort for the past 6 months. For each cohort, show me the percentage of users who were active in month 1, month 2, and month 3 after signup. Display this as a cohort retention table.
```
