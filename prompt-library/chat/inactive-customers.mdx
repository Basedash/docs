---
title: 'Inactive customer identification'
description: 'Find customers at risk of churning'
---

Identify customers who haven't engaged recently and may be at risk of churning. This prompt helps you proactively reach out to re-engage customers before they leave.

```
Find all customers who made at least one purchase but haven't made any purchases in the last 60 days. Show me their name, email, last purchase date, and total lifetime value. Sort by lifetime value descending.
```
