---
title: 'Product performance comparison'
description: 'Compare metrics across different products'
---

Compare key performance metrics across your product catalog to identify top performers and underperformers. This prompt helps you make data-driven decisions about inventory, marketing, and product development.

```
Compare all products by revenue, units sold, average selling price, and profit margin for the past quarter. Show me the top 15 products by revenue and highlight any products with declining sales compared to the previous quarter.
```
