Basedash lets you create beautiful charts and dashboards using natural language. Just describe what you want to see and Basedash will generate the rest.

## Signing up

Basedash is currently in beta. You can [join the waitlist here](https://charts.basedash.com/waitlist). We're actively onboarding new users every day.
